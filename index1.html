VISION


Empowering Lives, Celebrating Diversity

MISSION


At Minerva E-commerce Marketplace, we envision a world where every individual, regardless of their abilities or background, has the opportunity to thrive. Our mission is to create a platform that not only fosters economic independence but also celebrates the unique talents of individuals, especially those facing challenges.


ApnaGhar Foundation


Apnaghar Foundation is a haven for divyang, orphans, and needy women. Here, they find support, encouragement, and a sense of belonging. Many of our divyang individuals and groups passionately create handmade products, showcasing their skills and creativity.

Minerva E-commerce Marketplace


In response to the growing ease of online shopping, we proudly introduce Minerva E-commerce, a marketplace dedicated to handmade products. This platform brings together NGOs, Sakhi Mandals, and individual groups, offering a diverse range of over 500 daily-use items.

Concept


We believe in the powerful concept of "Help Yourself By Helping Others." When you make a purchase on our platform, you not only acquire a unique, handmade product but also contribute to the livelihoods of those who crafted it. Our mission is to bridge the gap between talented artisans and conscientious consumers.

Product Range


Explore a treasure trove of handmade items, including spices, millets, pickles, chutneys, papads, dry snacks, mukhvas, bags, hangings, handicrafts, jewelry, clothes, traditional items, and A to Z daily-use essentials. Every purchase tells a story of skill, resilience, and creativity.

Opportunities


Minerva E-commerce is not just a marketplace; it's a platform for artisans to showcase their talents. From blind individuals crafting doormats to weaving chairs and beds, we provide opportunities for everyone to contribute and be recognized.

Projects: Physically Challenged & Women Empowerment


We are actively involved in projects focused on empowering physically challenged individuals and promoting women's empowerment. Our initiatives aim to create a society where everyone can lead independent lives with dignity and respect.

Launch Date: International Women's Day - 8th March:


As we celebrate International Women's Day on 8th March, we choose this momentous occasion to launch Minerva E-commerce Marketplace. Join us in our mission to empower lives and make a positive impact in the world.
